Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as pages visited and actions taken, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Spendly service.
  • Display your expense history and summaries to you.
  • Send essential account-related emails (e.g. password reset).
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the app experience.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures — including encryption at rest and in transit — to protect your information from unauthorized access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Hosting providers — to serve the application and store data.
  • Font services — Google Fonts is loaded to render the app's typography. Google may collect limited usage data per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond as soon as possible.
